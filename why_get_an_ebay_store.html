Why get an eBay Store

Do you want to get most out of the business?

Are you looking for an all-in-one online business solution which will help you reach millions of buyers?

eBay Store is the answer!

eBay stores are the central shopping destination where your buyers will learn more about the products that you are listing. Along with helping you reach the right customer, an ebay store will also help in managing your business efficiently and building your brand.

Subscribing for an eBay store opens up access to more free listings and various tools that will help you succeed in your business. Crucially all your listings appear at one place – letting your buyers have more scope to make their choices.

Let’s understand a little more about eBay stores and how you can use it to your benefit to grow and achieve your sales goals.

Are you looking at listing more than 250+ listings every month, but listing fees are holding you back?

Well, then eBay store is the right solution for you.

Subscribing for an eBay store will allow you to enjoy several key benefits

Save on additional free listing & get lower final value fees

Drive buyer traffic to your listings and your eBay store

Get “Free Access**” to the “Terapeak Research Tool” for sourcing insights

Take a “Time Away” without loosing sales that impact your business

Get “Free Access**” to the “Terapeak Research Tool” for sourcing insights

Create your own brand and get an unique URL for your store to keep up with regular & new buyers

** Only applicable to Basic, Premium, Anchor and Enterprise Stores.
